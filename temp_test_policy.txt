Insurance Policy Document
Coverage Overview:
This policy covers hospitalization expenses up to Rs 5,00,000 per year.
Sub-limits: Room rent is capped at 1% of sum insured per day (Rs 5000/day).
Pre-existing diseases have a 24-month waiting period before coverage begins.
Maternity benefits: Rs 50,000 limit with a 9-month waiting period.
Exclusions: Cosmetic surgery, dental treatment, and self-inflicted injuries are not covered.
Claims Process: Intimate the insurer within 48 hours of hospitalization. Submit original bills.
Network Hospitals: Cashless treatment available at 10,000+ network hospitals.
